Technology and Information System

SECP1513 Technology and Information System introduce introduce us to information systems and technology (IS/IT) and its uses at home and work. By learning this course, we will study deeply about the topics hardware, software, network and communications. In this course, we have industrial visit to go Huawei, Petronas and UTM Digital which make us Get to know the corporate culture as early as possible, experience the working atmosphere and gain a deeper understanding of these companies and department. Besides, we also equipped with basic skills in handling PC installation.
