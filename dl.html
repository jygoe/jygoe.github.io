Digital Logic

SECR1013 Digital Logic introduces us to digital electronics which is the foundation of all microprocessor-based systems found in computers, robots, automobiles, and industrial control systems. It provides a broad overview of many important concepts, components, and tools. Students will get up-to-date coverage of digital fundamentals-from basic concepts to programmable logic devices. Laboratory experiments provide hands-on experience with the simulator software, actual devices and circuits studied in the classroom.

6. Reflection

For me, this course started very simply and was about gate logic. But by the fifth module, I felt it was getting harder and harder and I started to be unable to keep up with what the lecturer was teaching. After going back to my hostel room, I need to review it again before I can understand it. But later, when I didn't understand something in class, I would raise my hand and ask a question so that the lecturer could repeat it then I could understand better. Overall, this course is quite challenging for me, but I feel very accomplished after mastering it.
